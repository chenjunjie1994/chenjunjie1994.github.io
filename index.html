Biography

I obtained my Master of Philosophy degree from Department of Computer Science of Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU) in Nov 2019, under Prof. William K. Cheung's watchful eyes. Prior to that, I received my B.Eng. degree from Department of Computer Science, Jinan University (JNU) in 2016.

From September to December 2018, I was a research intern at RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project, Tokyo, working on discrete optimization with Dr. Takanori Maehara. From June to September 2019, I was a research intern at Tencent AI Lab, working on reinforcement learning with Dr. Bei Shi.
